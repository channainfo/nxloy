# Social Domain - Business Rules

**Last Updated**: 2025-11-09

## BR-S-001: Micro-Influencer Range
- 10,000 <= followers <= 50,000

## BR-S-002: Match Score Threshold
- matchScore >= 70 for partnership consideration

## BR-S-003: Bot Score Limit
- Reject if botScore > 20%

## BR-S-004: Commission Structure
- Standard: $5-10 per signup
- Platform fee: 20%
- Influencer receives: 80% of commission

## BR-S-005: Challenge Duration
- Min: 7 days, Max: 30 days

## BR-S-006: Team Size
- Min: 2 members, Max: 10 members

## BR-S-007: Challenge Participation
- Customer can join max 3 active challenges simultaneously
