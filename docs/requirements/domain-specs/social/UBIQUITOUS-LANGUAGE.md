# Social Domain - Ubiquitous Language

**Last Updated**: 2025-11-09

## Core Terms

**Micro-Influencer**: 10K-50K followers, higher engagement than macro-influencers (11x ROI)

**Match Score**: 0-100 rating of influencer-business fit (audience overlap: 35%, engagement: 25%, content alignment: 20%, bot score: 15%, follower tier: 5%)

**Audience Overlap**: Demographic match between influencer followers and business target audience

**Bot Score**: Percentage of fake followers (HypeAuditor API), reject if >20%

**Viral Challenge**: TikTok/Instagram challenge with hashtag, team competition, rewards

**Trend Detection**: AI scrapes trending hashtags/songs for challenge ideas

**Team Challenge**: 2-10 customers compete together (vs. individual)

**Leaderboard**: Real-time rankings by team progress

**Challenge Participation**: Joining challenge and submitting UGC

**ROI Prediction**: Expected signups/revenue before contacting influencer

**Automated DM Outreach**: GPT-4o generates personalized partnership invitation

**Commission Model**: Influencer earns $5-10 per signup (platform takes 20% fee)
